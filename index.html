The Challenge

Workload Growth Shouldn’t Mean CPA Burnout.

Finding qualified tax staff takes time you don't have. Generalist agencies don't understand CPA workflows, filing deadlines, or what full-time team integration actually means. You need capacity built around your firm — not a rotating contractor.

Hiring Takes Too Long

Recruiting and screening a qualified tax preparer takes weeks your firm doesn't have — especially heading into filing season.

Generic Agencies Miss the Mark

Most staffing firms don't understand Drake, CPA workflows, or the difference between a 1065 and a 1120-S. You end up training the people who were supposed to be ready.

Growth Creates Capacity Risk

Taking on more clients without the right team creates quality risk, missed deadlines, and partner burnout. Scaling requires people — fast.

The Process

From Discovery to Placed Staff — in Days, Not Months.

01 · DISCOVER

Discovery & Setup

We learn your firm — team size, workflow, software stack, the role you need, and what a successful placement looks like for you.

02 · SOURCE

Role Intake & Sourcing

We define the position, source from our pre-vetted candidate pool, and begin outreach to qualified professionals aligned with your requirements.

03 · SCREEN

Screening & Shortlist

Every candidate is screened for skills, communication, software fit, and CPA-firm workflow experience before you see a single name.

Placement & Onboarding

You select your hire. We support the transition with role expectations, software access checklist, and first-week follow-up to ensure a clean start.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through ptgteam.com and related PT Global web properties.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a request through our website, we collect your name, email address, phone number, firm name, and any information you provide in the message field. Candidates who apply through the talent pool form also provide role type, experience level, availability, and background information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, assess your staffing needs or candidate profile, and facilitate introductions between CPA firms and qualified tax professionals. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at contact@ptgteam.com.

4. Candidate Data

Candidate information submitted through the talent pool form is used exclusively to match qualified professionals with CPA and tax firms. We will not share your profile with any firm without your prior consent. All placements are made with your knowledge and approval.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us at contact@ptgteam.com. PT Global is based in Miami, FL.
