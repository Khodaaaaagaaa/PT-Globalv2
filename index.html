The Process

From First Conversation to Integrated Team Member

01 · DISCOVER

Discovery & Setup

We learn your firm — team size, workflow, software stack, the role you need, and what a successful placement looks like for you.

02 · SOURCE

Role Intake & Sourcing

We define the position, source from our pre-vetted candidate pool, and begin outreach to qualified professionals aligned with your requirements.

03 · SCREEN

Screening & Shortlist

Every candidate is screened for skills, communication, software fit, and CPA-firm workflow experience before you see a single name.

04 · PLACE

Placement & Onboarding

You select your hire. We support the transition with role expectations, software access checklist, and first-week follow-up to ensure a clean start.
