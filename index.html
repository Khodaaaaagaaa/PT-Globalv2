Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026
This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through ptgteam.com and related PT Global web properties.

1. Information We Collect

When you submit a request through our website, we collect your name, email address, phone number, firm name, and any information you provide in the message field. Candidates who apply through the talent pool form also provide role type, experience level, availability, and background information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your inquiry, assess your staffing needs or candidate profile, and facilitate introductions between CPA firms and qualified tax professionals. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at Hello@PTGTeam.com.

4. Candidate Data

Candidate information submitted through the talent pool form is used exclusively to match qualified professionals with CPA and tax firms. We will not share your profile with any firm without your prior consent.

5. Contact

For any questions regarding this Privacy Policy or your personal data, contact us at Hello@PTGTeam.com. PT Global is based in the USA.
