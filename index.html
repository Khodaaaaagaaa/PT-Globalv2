Finding qualified tax staff takes time you don't have. Generalist agencies don't understand CPA workflows, filing deadlines, or what full-time team integration actually means. You need capacity built around your firm — not a rotating contractor.

The Process

From First Conversation to Integrated Team Member

01 · DISCOVER

Discovery & Setup

We learn your firm — team size, workflow, software stack, the role you need, and what success looks like for you.

02 · SOURCE

Role Intake & Sourcing

We define the position, source from our pre-vetted candidate pool, and begin outreach to qualified professionals.

03 · SCREEN

Screening & Shortlist

Every candidate is screened for skills, communication, software fit, and CPA-firm workflow experience before you see a single name.

04 · PLACE

Placement & Onboarding

You select your hire. We support the transition with role expectations, software access checklist, and first-week follow-up.
